# FS25_FleetView

A quality-of-life mod for Farming Simulator 25 that overhauls the default vehicle switching system. 

Stop blindly pressing the `Tab` key to cycle through your entire farm! **FS25_FleetView** replaces the vanilla tab-to-switch behavior with an interactive UI, giving you a complete overview of your fleet and letting you jump into the exact tractor you need in one click.

---

## üåü Features

* **Visual Vehicle Selection:** Replaces the default sequential `Tab` switch with a comprehensive graphical user interface (GUI).
* **Live Fleet Map:** View the exact real-time location of all your owned vehicles on an integrated map.
* **One-Click Teleport:** Simply click on a vehicle from the list or the map to instantly enter the cabin.
* **Time Saver:** Perfect for large farms with extensive fleets, eliminating the frustration of cycling past the vehicle you wanted.

---

## üéÆ How to Use

1. Load into your farm.
2. Press the **`Tab`** key (default vehicle switch key).
3. The FleetView GUI will open on your screen.
4. Locate your desired vehicle via the text list or the visual map.
5. Click on it to instantly enter the vehicle. 
6. Press `Esc` or `Tab` again to close the menu without switching.
